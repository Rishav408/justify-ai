Explainability Core

The backend is structured around four auditable pillars. Each response includes machine-readable evidence for moderation decisions.

Lexicon

Severity + Spans

Language-specific term matching with severity weights and highlighted spans.

Causality

Cause ? Effect

Rule-based causal extraction that explains narrative progression.

Bias

Demographic Audit

Bias co-occurrence scoring across religion, gender, ethnicity, and more.

Risk

Composite Score

0–100 risk level with breakdown across lexical, causal, and bias signals.

Pipeline Stages

Seven phases power every backend response, from schema to dashboard.

1. Schema + Language Coverage
2. Lexicon Matching
3. Causality Extraction
4. Bias Auditing
5. Risk Scoring
6. Frontend Wiring
7. QA + Validation
