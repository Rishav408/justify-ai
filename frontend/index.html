Platform Features

Lexicon Discovery

Automatically identify and extract hate speech terms from multilingual text using advanced NLP techniques. Build comprehensive lexicons with severity scoring and contextual understanding across Indian regional languages.

Causal Analysis

Uncover cause-and-effect relationships within hate narratives. Visualize how different elements connect and influence each other through interactive causal graphs, revealing the structure of harmful discourse.

Bias Auditing

Measure and quantify bias across multiple dimensions including religion, gender, and ethnicity. Get detailed metrics and visual representations to understand the nature and intensity of bias in analyzed content.
