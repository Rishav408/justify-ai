About Justify.AI

Explainable multilingual hate analysis for the real world.

Justify.AI is built for transparency-first moderation workflows. We combine classical NLP, interpretable modeling, and rigorous data validation to surface why a piece of text is classified as hate, offensive, or safe.

Mission & Principles

We focus on explainability over opacity. Every decision must be traceable to lexical evidence, causal structure, and bias indicators.
