About Justify.AI

Advancing explainable AI for multilingual hate speech detection

Project Purpose

Justify.AI is an advanced research project focused on developing explainable AI systems for detecting and analyzing hate speech in multilingual contexts, with a particular emphasis on Indian regional languages. The platform combines state-of-the-art natural language processing techniques with explainability frameworks to provide transparent, interpretable insights into hate speech patterns.

Our mission is to create tools that not only identify harmful content but also explain the reasoning behind classifications, enabling researchers, policymakers, and content moderators to make informed decisions. By supporting multiple Indian languages including Hindi, Bhojpuri, Marathi, and Marwari, we address the critical need for culturally-aware hate speech detection in diverse linguistic communities.

Research Background

This project emerged from the growing need to address online hate speech in multilingual environments, particularly in the Indian context where multiple languages and dialects coexist. Traditional hate speech detection systems often lack transparency and struggle with low-resource languages, making them inadequate for real-world deployment.

Justify.AI builds upon recent advances in explainable AI, causal inference, and multilingual NLP to create a comprehensive analysis framework. The system employs three complementary approaches:

  • Lexicon-based Analysis: Automated discovery and scoring of hate speech terms with contextual understanding
  • Causal Narrative Extraction: Identification of cause-and-effect relationships within harmful discourse
  • Bias Auditing: Multi-dimensional measurement of bias across religion, gender, and ethnicity

Our research contributes to the broader field of responsible AI by demonstrating how explainability can be integrated into hate speech detection systems without sacrificing performance.
