Mission dossier

Explainable Multilingual AI for Human Safety

Justify.AI was founded on the principle that AI decisions shouldn't be black boxes, especially when managing human discourse across dialects.

Our Core Values

Transparency

We believe in "Explainable Outputs" — showing the lexical, causal, and bias signals that lead to every classification.

Linguistic Equity

Giving regional Indian languages like Bhojpuri and Marwari the same level of scientific rigor as English and Hindi.

Justify.AI

Advanced multilingual hate speech detection with transparent, explainable AI insights across lexical, causal, and bias dimensions.
