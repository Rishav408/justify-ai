The 17 Dimensional Schema

Text & Label

The core linguistic node and its primary classification (Non-Hate, Hate, Offensive, Health Issue).

Severity Metric

Weighted intensity from None to Severe, determining the actionable risk level.

Contextual Flags

Boolean indicators for Implicitness and Sarcasm, handling nuanced linguistic hidden layers.

Target Taxonomy

Mapping to specific Target Group (Religion, Gender, Caste) and Target Type (Individual, Community).

Behavioral Tone

Classification of tone (Aggressive, Dismissive, Sarcastic) and underlying Emotion (Anger, Fear, Disgust).

Environmental Metadata

Recording is_sarcasm, profanity_count, platform (YouTube, Twitter), domain, and geographic Region.
